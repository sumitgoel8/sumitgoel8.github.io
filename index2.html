Brief Bio

Hi! I am a fifth-year PhD student in economics at California Institute of Technology. My research interests are in microeconomic theory and my advisor is Federico Echenique.

Before coming to Caltech, I received a Masters in Economics from Indian Statistical Institute Delhi and a B.Tech. in Computer Science from Delhi Technological University.

I am also an instructor at Econschool, an initiative to help undergraduate students in India interested in pursuing higher studies in Economics.
